Besides our contribution, we are beyond excited to hear about your experiences with technicalicities! Join our community by submitting your experiences via "Forum" so you can help other adventurers experience their best travel! ~

Go to Forum

See the Signs!

Inform yourself about the traffic rules of the country or countries you are traveling to. For example the speed limit, and traffic signs!

~ Anne, via Forum

Drive Legally!

Make sure you check beforehand if you have the right driver’s license to legally drive a camper. If not, make sure you do the appropriate courses to get it!

~ Pete, via Forum

Check, Double Check!

Check your equipment before you start to travel with it. If there is anything wrong, you are able to fix it more easily at home!

~ Louis, via Forum


Practice Makes Perfect!

If you are going to travel to the mountains or other places with uneven roads, make sure you practice enough with driving the Camper Van to feel more relaxed while traveling

~ Sam, via Forum

Plan 'Till You Drop!

Plan your trip before so you know which campsites you will be visiting and therefore know the facilities you have to provide yourself or which ones you can use at the campsites for example electricity and plumming

~ Kate, via Forum

Close 'Em All!

Before you start to drive again with your camper, remember to close all the windows, panorama windows, and remove the TV satelitte to protect it from surrounding weather and nature while driving!

~ Sarah, via Forum
